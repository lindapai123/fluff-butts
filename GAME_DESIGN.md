## 🐕 Dog Characters

### Memphis — Golden Retriever ⭐ (Starting Dog)
*Inspired by Emma's real golden retriever*

- **Personality:** Fun, bouncy, enthusiastic
- **Difficulty:** Easy / Beginner
- **Special Moves:**
  - 🦌 **Deer Hop** — bounces extra high over obstacles instead of going around them
  - 🐺 **Howl Power-Up** — tap the howl button and Memphis lets out an "Awwwooo Woooo!" that scares obstacles out of the way

---

### Lincoln — Labrador 🔒 (Unlockable / Hard Mode)
*Inspired by Emma's real lab*

- **Personality:** Feisty, stubborn, opinionated
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Special Traits:**
  - 😤 **Stubborn Mode** — sometimes just stops mid-course and won't move until you drop multiple bones right in front of him
  - 💧 **Hates Water** — requires twice as many bones to move through water sections; shakes his head and resists
  - ⚡ **Feisty Sprint** — once he finally gets going, he's very fast and hard to steer
- **Best used for:** The swimming course (hard mode)

## 🎮 Core Gameplay

### Obstacle Courses
- Player taps the screen to drop a bone 🦴
- Dog automatically runs toward the nearest bone
- Navigate past rocks, hurdles, water, and other obstacles
- Reach the finish line 🏁 to complete the course

### Star Rating System
| Stars | Performance |
|-------|-------------|
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Fast, few treats used |
| ⭐⭐ | Average time, moderate treats |
| ⭐ | Slow or lots of treats used |
| 💩 | Lincoln swam |

## ✂️ The Grooming Mini-Game

*Emma's design: "more wet and take longer to blow dry, a.k.a. take care of"*

After every course, you groom your dog's fluffy butt. How long it takes depends on performance:

| Result | Butt Condition | Grooming Time |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| ⭐⭐⭐ Perfect run | Clean & fluffy | Quick fluff — done instantly! |
| ⭐⭐ Okay run | A little dirty | Needs some brushing |
| ⭐ Rough run | Muddy & soggy | Blow dry required 💨 |
| 💀 Lincoln swam | Completely soaked | MAXIMUM blow dry time 😂 |

- Blow dryer sound gets more dramatic the worse the run was 🎵
- Grooming tools to unlock: brushes, bows, ribbons

## 🎵 Audio Ideas

- Background music: playful, upbeat
- Treat pickup: satisfying crunch/pop
- Memphis howl: *"Awwwoooo Woooooo"* 🐺
- Lincoln refusing water: grumpy bark + head shake
- Blow dryer: gets louder/more dramatic with worse performance
